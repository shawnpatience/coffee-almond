What it is

Ground coffee meets sweet almond oil.

Our Coffee & Almond Organic Scrub is made with 100% natural ingredients - ground coffee, sweet almond oil and nothing your skin doesn't need. No fillers. No synthetics. Just a scrub that genuinely works.

Use it in the shower on your face, body, elbows and knees. The coarse coffee grounds exfoliate while the almond oil nourishes. Rinse to reveal skin that glows for days - not hours. Suitable for all genders, all ages, all skin types.

The Science

Six reasons your skin will thank you.

Coffee and almond oil aren't random choices - they're a powerhouse combination with real, documented skin benefits.

Balances Skin pH

Coffee's polyphenols regulate your skin's pH - less sebum, fewer breakouts and skin that stays smooth and hydrated all day.

Reduces Cellulite

Caffeine draws excess water from skin while the scrubbing motion boosts circulation - visibly plumping and smoothing dimpled areas.

Boosts Collagen

Antioxidants in coffee stimulate collagen production, smoothing fine lines and restoring elasticity for firmer-looking skin over time.

De-Puffs Eyes

Caffeine tightens skin, fights free radicals and visibly reduces dark circles and morning puffiness around the eye area.

Almond Oil Brightens

Niacinamide in almond oil fades age spots, blemishes and dark circles for noticeably more radiant, even-toned skin.

It is lightweight, non-comedogenic and rich in Vitamin E, which helps hydrate, reduce inflammation and improves the complexion.

Soothes Eczema & Psoriasis

Anti-bacterial and anti-fungal properties of almond oil soothe irritated, inflamed skin while locking in moisture.

How to use it

Two steps to glowing skin.

Step 01

Scrub in the Shower

Apply in circular motions on wet skin - face, body, elbows, knees. Let the coffee grounds do the work. Rinse thoroughly to reveal impossibly smooth, glowing skin.

Step 02

Pat Dry, Let it Work

The sweet almond oil from the scrub stays in your skin after rinsing. Pat dry - don't rub - to lock in the nourishment. Your skin is already working. Now continue with the gua sha.
