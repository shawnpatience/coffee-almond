Grounds for Everything

It started with a coffee shop.

A story of waste turned to wonder, a mother's determination, and skin that finally found its answer.

The beginning

A question that kept coming up.

In 2016, Jodie helped a dear friend at the launch of his coffee shop called I Love Coffee - and somewhere between the roasting beans, the laughter and that intoxicating smell that makes everything feel a little more alive, a question kept coming up: what happens to all these grounds?

At the same time, life at home was asking its own questions. Her husband and young son were both struggling with eczema on their faces - red, irritated, unhappy skin. As any determined mother does, Jodie started researching. Then experimenting. Then researching some more.

Weeks passed. Different oils. Different textures. Different types of containers stacked up on the kitchen counter.

The discovery

And then - it worked.

Coffee & Almond Organic Scrub was born from that very specific kind of love: the kind that starts with a problem and refuses to stop until it finds an answer. The kind that turns waste into wonder. The kind that makes a mother try one more time.

"The kind that starts with a problem and refuses to stop until it finds an answer. The kind that turns waste into wonder."

The launch

Small. Beautiful. Real.

The launch was small and beautiful - a limited budget, a table at a market and a product made with complete conviction. The reviews poured in. The jars flew off the table. Word spread the way good things do - person to person, skin to skin.

Life happened, as it does. The economy shifted. A pandemic arrived uninvited. The world got complicated and the side hustle had to breathe.

But the passion never left. Neither did the belief.

Still here

We found our way back.

Because when something genuinely works, when it starts on your own family's skin - you don't give up on it. You just find your way back.

We're still here. Still small-batch. Still obsessed with what we put in the jar. And we're here because of one simple truth that hasn't changed since 2016.

"If your coffee wakes you up in the morning imagine what it can do for your skin!"

Welcome to Coffee & Almond. We're so glad you found us. ☕
