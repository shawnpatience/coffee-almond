Why each piece
earns its place.

Every item in this bundle is working hard for your skin, your sleep, and your nervous system. These aren't luxury extras — they're tools with serious science behind them. Here's exactly what they do.

Balances Skin PH

Coffee's polyphenols regulate your skin's PH — less sebum, fewer breakouts, and skin that stays smooth and hydrated all day.

Reduces Cellulite

Caffeine draws excess water from skin while the scrubbing motion boosts circulation — visibly plumping and smoothing dimpled areas.

Boosts Collagen

Antioxidants in coffee stimulate collagen production, smoothing fine lines and restoring elasticity for firmer-looking skin over time.

De-Puffs Eyes

Caffeine tightens skin, fights free radicals, and visibly reduces dark circles and morning puffiness around the eye area.

Almond Oil Brightens

Niacinamide in almond oil fades age spots, blemishes, and dark circles for noticeably more radiant, even-toned skin.

Soothes Eczema & Psoriasis

Anti-bacterial and anti-fungal properties of almond oil soothe irritated, inflamed skin while locking in moisture.

Lymphatic Drainage

The scraping motion physically moves lymph fluid, flushing toxins and reducing overnight puffiness. A visibly slimmer face in minutes.

Sculpts & Defines

Regular use along the jaw and cheekbones trains facial muscles and enhances your natural bone structure — a subtle but cumulative lifting effect.

Releases Jaw Tension

Targets the masseter and frontalis muscles — the ones that hold stress. Relieves clenched jaws and furrowed brows. Faces relax visibly.

Boosts Circulation

Increases blood flow to the skin's surface, giving you that natural post-workout flush — and with it, a healthy, lasting glow.

Anti-Bacterial Steel

Unlike jade or rose quartz, stainless steel is non-porous — it cannot harbour bacteria. Easy to sanitise, hygienic every use, kind to acne-prone skin.

Enhances Absorption

Used over a serum or facial oil, the gu'sha presses actives deeper into skin — your serums finally working as hard as they should.

From the Fridge or Freezer

Stainless steel holds cold beautifully. Chill your gu'sha 20–30 min in the fridge or 10 min in the freezer for full cryo-therapy benefits:

  • Instantly tightens and firms skin
  • Dramatically reduces morning puffiness
  • Constricts capillaries to calm redness & rosacea
  • Stimulates collagen via thermal shock
  • Locks in serums more effectively
  • Anti-inflammatory — ideal after sun exposure

At Room Temperature

Steel naturally feels cool. For a warming effect, run briefly under warm water before use:

  • Opens pores for deeper product penetration
  • Relaxes tight facial muscles more easily
  • Ideal for dry, tight or winter-stressed skin
  • More comfortable for those new to gua sha
  • Enhances circulation without the intensity of cold

Calms the Nervous System

Total Light Blackout

Complete darkness triggers melatonin production. You fall asleep faster and sleep deeper — your body gets the biological signal to rest.

Relieves Headaches & Migraines

Gentle pressure on key points around the eyes and temples reduces tension headaches. Blocks light that worsens migraines. Works warm or cold.

Faster Sleep Onset

The combination of darkness and pressure quiets an overactive mind. You stop mentally rehearsing your day and start drifting within minutes.

Perfect for Travel

Blocks light on planes, in hotels, during night shifts. The weight keeps it in place — no slipping, no straps digging in. Portable self-care anywhere.

Reduces Puffiness & Dark Circles

The gentle pressure improves circulation in the delicate eye area. Use it chilled for an immediate, visible de-puffing effect.

How it All Fits Together

Your evening ritual,
step by step.
