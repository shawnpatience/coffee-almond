Surgical-Grade Steel Anti-Bacterial Cold & Warm Sculpts & Lifts All Skin Types
What it does

Not jade. Not quartz. Surgical steel.

Unlike porous stone tools that harbour bacteria, our gua sha is crafted from surgical-grade stainless steel - the same material used in operating theatres. Non-porous, hygienic, and impossible to contaminate. It cleans in seconds.

Use it after your scrub on damp skin with a serum or facial oil. Five minutes along the jaw, cheekbones, forehead, and under-eyes. You'll see a visible difference immediately - and a cumulative one over weeks.

Temperature Guide

Cold or warm - your choice.

Steel holds temperature beautifully. Use it chilled for cryo benefits or warm for deep muscle release. Both work. Both glow.

From the Fridge or Freezer

Chill your gua sha 20–30 min in the fridge or 10 min in the freezer for full cryo-therapy benefits:

  • Instantly tightens and firms skin
  • Dramatically reduces morning puffiness
  • Constricts capillaries to calm redness & rosacea
  • Stimulates collagen via thermal shock
  • Locks in serums more effectively
  • Anti-inflammatory - ideal after sun exposure

At Room Temperature

Steel naturally feels cool. For a warming effect, run briefly under warm water before use:

  • Opens pores for deeper product penetration
  • Relaxes tight facial muscles more easily
  • Ideal for dry, tight or winter-stressed skin
  • More comfortable for those new to gua sha
  • Enhances circulation without the intensity of cold
